About Us

New D.V.A.(Earlier it was known as DVA) is Primary School in Dashrathpur, Munger. New DVA established in 2022. Due to CORONA we have to shut down our school(DVA) but now because of immense love and trust of parents and students we have again started a School - New D.V.A. we hope you also give same love and support as DVA. We believe in Quality Education and New DVA Team and our Teachers always try to give the quality education that are useful for not only qualifying Exams but also building the base..
